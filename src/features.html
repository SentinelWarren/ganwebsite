Features

Collect subscribers by importing existing contacts, use a subscription form on your website or through links on social media.

It shouldn't be hard to create beautiful newsletters, that's why we created our block editor with drag-and-drop functionality.

Track your results

Get relevant and straightforward statistics with our reports. See who opened, how many clicked on a certain link and much more.
